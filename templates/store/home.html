About Us

Our goal is to be the most trusted and largest vegetarian family restaurant by delivering delight to our guests through our dedicated team members. We want to be the preferred family restaurant for our customers, offering fast, fresh and tasty food in clean, safe, hygienic surroundings and achieve highest standards of guest delight. For the employees, we want to be the employer of choice, to grow profitably through efficient means and maximization of revenues while conducting ourselves with social responsibility.

Nothing helps a person understand a culture better than exploring the region’s street food. In India, street food industry protects traditional recipes that run through ancestry lines. Street food provides a convenient diet for many people in developing countries like India . Food stalls are often owned and handled by a family.

We are one such family situated in Delhi, who wanted to pass on the taste of our ancestral recipes to various people. Our parents started a small roadside dhaba named “Reet” in 1960. They worked in this dhaba for 40 years to ear bread and butter for the family. His son Reet, having left his education midway, decided to take over the dhaba to continue earning for his family. In the year 2004-05, Gurmeet first changed the name of the dhaba from “Reet” to “The Retro Affair” to stay in line with the changing times.

Accepting changes and by making the necessary changes with time, though we cannot compete with the marketing strategies of big delivery companies, we are sure of the word-of-mouth marketing of our restaurant through our esteemed customers. With love of our customers and blessings of almighty, “The Retro Affair” has already won the hearts of the city.

We follow all Food Safety Standards and are taking all necessary precautions to fight against viruses like Covid. We are rigorously following the hourly hand-wash process for hygiene. In-house training programmes are regularly conducted for staff. All our food items are checked four times a day for appropriate temperatures. Also our employees are being educated about this pandemic and are told to wear masks and maintain distance.
